Clue #5

The fifth and the final clue is a bit tricky. For this clue, there are FIVE possible locations where the clue can appear. The fifth clue is the murderer's van and there are five possible locations the van can spawn. You need to check all of these location until you come across the van. The possible spawn locations are listed below.

All possible spawn locations

Possible Spawn Location 1

The first possible location is near Fort Zancudo. You will find it near Lago Zancudo river and swamp area as shown in the map.

Possible Spawn Location 2

The second possible spawn location for the fifth clue is near Mount Gordo area. You can find it on the side of the railway as marked by a red circle on the picture above.

Possible Spawn Location 3

The third spawn location is at Palmer-Taylor Power Station near a big tank. You can find the van beside the red tank.

Possible Spawn Location 4

Fourth possible location for the van to spawn is underneath the bridge/highway located near San Chianski Mountain Range. Right beside the railway track.

Possible Spawn Location 5

The final possible spawn location is at Raton Canyon by the river. The van will be in between the trees overlooking the river.

Los Santos Slasher Confrontation

After you've collected all the clues, you will receive a text message from the killer themselves.

The killer send you a message threatening you. The message says:

I'm sick of you sticking your nose in where it doesn't belong! Now I'm not going to lie... what happens next... won't be nice for you

After you receive the text message, the slasher will come after you. However, there are certain requirements in order for the slasher to spawn for you. They are:

  • You need to be in the Blaine County area. It doesn't matter where specifically, but it has to be in Blaine County.
  • The slasher only spawns at night in between 7 P.M. and 5 A.M.
  • You need to be on foot for the slasher to spawn for you.

Blaine county airfield is an area of recommendation. It is an open area with good visibility. The slasher will spawn outside your field of view with a machete.
